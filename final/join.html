Non-Profit tier is the basic bronze tier, but is only allowed for those who run a non-profit orgamization.

Bronze tier grants you membership to our chamber - you can vote in any decisions, and your opinions are valued and considered.

Silver tier has the basic bronze membership included, and also includes access to chamber luncheons, plus you're given a VIP title which is purely cosmetic.

Gold tier grants bronze and silver-tier benefits, plus a VVIP title, which lets you vote twice.
