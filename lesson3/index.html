Weather Site Plan

Site Name

Oh No, Weather!

Site Purpose

The purpose of "Oh No, Weather!" is to provide information about when strong weather might disrupt a person's plans, and allow them to plan around this new information. This includes providing upcoming high and low temperatures, weather descriptions like "sunny" or "rainy", and having statements like "perfect for a picnic" or "stay indoors".
